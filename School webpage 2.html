Junior years (7-10)

The junior years comprise of year 7 to year 10. Stage 4 comprises of years 7 and 8 and years 9 and 10 is stage 5.

In both stages English, Mathematics, Science and HSIE (geography and History) are composulary for students to learn.

In year 8 students are given the opportunity to choose their own Language subject they want, for example French, Mandarin and Japanese.

In year 8 Students also get to choose their subject electives for years 9 and 10. These include Commerce, Music, Drama, IST, Multimedia, STEM,

Visual Arts, French, Mandarin, Japanese, Agriculture, Food Technology, Textiles and Wood Technology. At the end of year 10, students decide their

senior subject for years 11 and 12.


Senior years (11-12)

The senior years consists of years 11 and 12 and are classifyed as Stage 6. Year 11 is also known as the preliminary year before the HSC (year 12)

and the only subject that is composulary in both years 11 and 12 is English. In years 11 and 12 the subjects available include English, Mathematics, Chemisty, Biology,

Physics, Business Studies, Legal Studies, Economics, History, Geography, Music, Visual Arts, Wood Technolgy, Languages, and many more Subjects.

In year 12, students complete the Higher School Certificate (HSC) which determines their next stage in their education eg University.
